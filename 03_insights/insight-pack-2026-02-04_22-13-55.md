- Both Libellia FR and Studio IT users report that pediatric patient syntheses do not make clinical status or priorities immediately clear ([sig_2026_02_03_001], [sig_2026_02_03_101], [sig_2026_02_04_006]).
- High cognitive load from excessive or poorly prioritized information is a recurring pain point across both products ([sig_2026_02_03_003], [sig_2026_02_03_103], [sig_2026_02_04_007]).
- Pediatric-specific data (growth, percentiles, vaccinations) are not sufficiently prominent, especially in Libellia FR ([sig_2026_02_03_005], [sig_2026_02_03_006], [sig_2026_02_03_007]).
- Users in both countries want clearer clinical alerts and more visible vaccination status ([sig_2026_02_04_019], [sig_2026_02_04_020], [sig_2026_02_03_007], [sig_2026_02_03_105]).
- Navigation and workflow guidance are suboptimal, with requests for more guided consultation flows and easier document retrieval ([sig_2026_02_04_013], [sig_2026_02_04_014], [sig_2026_02_04_011], [sig_2026_02_04_012]).
