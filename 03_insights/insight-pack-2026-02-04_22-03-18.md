# Insight Analysis (Context-aware)

## 1) Executive Summary

- Both Libellia FR and Studio IT users report difficulty quickly assessing pediatric patient status from the synthesis view ([sig_2026_02_03_001], [sig_2026_02_03_101]).
- Key pediatric data (growth, percentiles, vaccinations) are not sufficiently prioritized or visible in patient views ([sig_2026_02_03_005], [sig_2026_02_03_006], [sig_2026_02_03_105]).
- High cognitive load is caused by excessive or poorly prioritized information, affecting both FR and IT users ([sig_2026_02_03_003], [sig_2026_02_03_103]).
- Clinical priorities and alerts are not prominent enough, especially during first or urgent visits ([sig_2026_02_03_004], [sig_2026_02_03_104]).
